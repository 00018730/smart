Writing Test Instructions

The Writing test consists of two tasks.

  • Task 1: Describe visual information (150 words).
  • Task 2: Write an essay responding to an argument or problem (250 words).
  • You have 60 minutes to complete both tasks.
  • You should spend about 20 minutes on Task 1 and 40 minutes on Task 2.
  • You can switch freely between tasks.
  • Answers are saved automatically.

Once the test starts, it cannot be paused.
